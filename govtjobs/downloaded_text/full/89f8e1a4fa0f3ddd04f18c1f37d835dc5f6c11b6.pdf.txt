CEN 01/2018 (Assistant Loco Pilot and Technicians) 

Second Stage CBT 

The result processing of the First Stage CBT is in final stages. List of candidates 

shortlisted for Second Stage CBT for each RRB, based on their Merit and Post 

Preference, is likely to be published on or before 5th November, 2018 along with 

the actual marks and normalized score of the candidates.  

 

Candidates are advised to note that the Second Stage CBT is likely to start from 

19th November 2018.  

 

The syllabus for various Exam Trades has been published on the official websites 

of RRBs through a link. Candidates can download their exam trade syllabus by 

clicking on the relevant trade name.  

 

Candidates are further advised to regularly visit official websites of RRBs for 

latest updates.
