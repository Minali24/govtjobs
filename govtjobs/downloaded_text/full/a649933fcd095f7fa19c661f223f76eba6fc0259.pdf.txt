     Extension of date upto 31-12-2018for correction of Bank account details 
for Refund of Examination Fee 

 
 
 
 
 
Railway Recruitment Boards have initiated Refund of Examination Fee to the candidates who had attended 
the First Stage Computer Based Test (CBT) for ALP/Technicians against CEN 01/2018.  
 
Candidates whose refund transaction has failed due to incorrect bank account details have been intimated 
regarding the failure of refund through SMS and email. 
 
Initially, the last date for correction of Bank account was given as 25-12-18. However to facilitate the 
candidates to complete the bank account correction, t 
he last date for bank account correction has been extended to 31-12-18 and accordingly the Bank account 
correction link shall be live till 31-12-18. 
 
Candidates are advised to make use of this final opportunity to correct the Bank details to enable 

refund.While filling the Bank Account information, please ensure that the Bank Account Number and IFSC 

Code entered are correct. 

Please note that this is the last opportunity and therefore you are advised to furnish correct details of Bank 
Account Number and IFSC code. Once you submit the bank details, further editing of bank details is NOT 
permissible and RRB shall not be responsible for further failure of refund, if any, and no correspondence 
will be entertained in this regard.
